Why This Course

A subject is only as deep as the teacher who brings it to you.

कबीर, तुलसी, या निराला को केवल पढ़ना नहीं है — उन्हें सुनना है, उनसे बात करनी है। परीक्षा तो बाद की बात है। — Vikas Sir · Orientation Lecture

Hindi Literature has quietly become one of UPSC's most scoring optionals — 270+ is not unheard of. But the candidates who reach those marks rarely do so by memorising a booklist. They do so by developing a sensibility: the ability to read Kabir's dohas as living theology, Premchand's stories as political texts, Muktibodh's imagery as psychology.

This course is built around that conviction. Every lecture begins with the text, not the textbook. Every module reconstructs a period — its politics, its spiritual climate, its controversies — before it names a single poet. And every answer-writing session is evaluated by faculty who have read the original works, not summaries of them.

What you will leave with is not a completed syllabus. It is a reading practice that will serve you in the Mains, in the interview, and — if you clear — in the decades of service afterward.

Two papers. Sixteen modules. One arc.

Common Questions

Before you enroll.

Do I need a Hindi Literature background from college? +
No. A comfortable reading knowledge of Hindi is enough. The course builds from first principles — including the history of the language itself — and the pace is set for someone serious about the optional, but without formal training in it.
In what language are the lectures delivered? +
Entirely in Hindi. Primary texts are read in their original. Technical terms and critical framing also come in Hindi, with English glosses provided in notes where helpful.
How does the answer evaluation work? +
You submit handwritten answers through the app. Faculty — each of whom has either cleared Mains with this optional or holds an advanced degree in Hindi literature — return them within 72 hours with written feedback, a rubric-based score, and line-level comments.
What happens if I miss a live session? +
All live sessions are recorded and uploaded within 24 hours, with chapter markers and a searchable transcript. You also retain access to the full recording library for 18 months.
Can I get a refund if the course isn't for me? +
Yes. A full refund is available within the first 7 days of the cohort start date, no questions asked. After that, partial refunds are handled case-by-case.
Is this course aligned with the latest UPSC syllabus? +
Yes. The curriculum is mapped to the official UPSC Mains Hindi Literature syllabus (Paper I and Paper II) and is reviewed each year against the latest notification and previous year questions.
